Stew Recipie

Below is a Recipie that will forever cahnge your outlook on life you will forever be a better person

Ingredients
  1. Beef Potjies
  2. Tomato
  3. Onions
  4. Worster sauce
  5. Beef cube
  6. Carrots
Steps
  1. Prepare the Ingredients: Dice the onions, carrots, and tomatoes. Crumble the beef cube into a small bowl for later use.
  2. Preheat the Potjie Pot: Place your potjie pot over medium heat on an open flame or stovetop. Add a bit of oil or butter to prevent sticking.
  3. Brown the Beef: Add the beef chunks to the pot and brown them on all sides. This helps seal in the flavor.
  4. Add Onions and Carrots: Toss in the onions and carrots. Sauté them with the beef for 5–7 minutes until softened.
  5. Incorporate the Tomatoes: Stir in the diced tomatoes. Let them cook down to form a rich base for about 10 minutes.
  6. Season and Add Beef Cube: Add the crumbled beef cube and stir well. This will enhance the depth of the flavors.
  7. Pour in Worcestershire Sauce: Add a generous splash of Worcestershire sauce (adjust to taste). Stir to coat all the ingredients.
  8. Simmer Slowly: Lower the heat and cover the pot. Let it simmer gently for about 1.5–2 hours, stirring occasionally to prevent sticking.
  9. Check and Adjust: Periodically check the potjie. If it's too dry, add a splash of water or beef stock. Taste and adjust seasoning if needed.
  10. Serve and Enjoy: Once the beef is tender and flavors are well combined, serve your Beef Potjie hot with rice, bread, or pap (traditional South African porridge).
