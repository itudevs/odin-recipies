Ingredients
  1. Aromat
  2. eggs
  3. cooking oil
Steps
  1. Prepare the Ingredients: Crack 2â€“3 eggs into a bowl. Add a pinch of salt and pepper to taste. Whisk the eggs until well combined.
  2. Heat the Pan: Place a non-stick pan over medium heat. Add a small amount of butter or oil to coat the surface.
  3. Cook the Eggs: Pour the whisked eggs into the pan. Let them sit for a few seconds without stirring.
  4. Stir Gently: Use a spatula or wooden spoon to gently stir and fold the eggs. Keep stirring occasionally to create soft curds.
  5. Remove from Heat: Once the eggs are slightly undercooked (still shiny but not runny), remove the pan from heat. The residual heat will finish cooking them.
  6. Serve and Enjoy: Transfer the eggs to a plate and serve immediately. Optionally, garnish with fresh herbs like chives or parsley.
