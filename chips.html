Chips Recipie

Below is a Recipie that will forever cahnge your outlook on life you will forever be a better person

Ingredients
  1. Salt
  2. Potatos
  3. cooking oil
Steps
  1. Prepare the Potatoes: Wash and peel the potatoes. Cut them into even-sized strips (about 1/4 inch thick for classic fries)
  2. Soak the Potatoes: Place the potato strips in a bowl of cold water for 30 minutes. This removes excess starch, making the chips crispier.
  3. Cook the Eggs: Pour the whisked eggs into the pan. Let them sit for a few seconds without stirring.
  4. Dry the Potatoes: Drain the soaked potatoes and pat them completely dry with a clean kitchen towel or paper towels.
  5. Fry the Chips (Twice): Heat oil in a deep pan or fryer to about 150°C (300°F). Fry the potato strips in batches for 5–7 minutes until soft but not browned. Remove and drain on paper towels. Increase the oil temperature to 180°C (350°F) and fry the potatoes again until golden and crispy
  6. Season and Serve: Remove the chips from the oil and drain them on fresh paper towels. Sprinkle with salt (or your favorite seasoning) while they're still hot. Serve immediately.
